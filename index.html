Peanut Curry

The peanut curry is a delicious meal for everyone to send it hard on! Not for peeps alergic to peanuts because this ting is going to be exploding with peanuts! Have it with rice or serve in fresh hollowed out green peppers. Boom shaka laka ting ting!

Ingredients

  1. Tofu
  2. Peanut butter
  3. Dried chillies
  4. Green pepper
  5. Honey
  6. Soy Sauce
